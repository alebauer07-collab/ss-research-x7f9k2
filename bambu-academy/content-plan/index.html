Got a lot of questions after the last email.

 

Most common one: "What do you actually cover in 4 weeks?"

 

Fair question. Here's the breakdown:

 

WEEK 1: FOUNDATION

Movement mechanics. Not just "how" but why bodies move the way they do. You'll never look at a squat the same way again.

 

WEEK 2: COMMUNICATION

This is where most coaches fail. You know what's wrong. You just can't get it from your head into their body. We fix that.

 

WEEK 3: GROUP DYNAMICS

Running a class isn't the same as coaching 1:1. Session flow. Energy management. How to see 12 people at once. The stuff no one teaches.

 

WEEK 4: BUSINESS & INTEGRATION

Programming. Pricing. Building a client base that doesn't require you to work 60 hours a week. Plus putting it all together with real coaching scenarios.

 

Every day: classroom in the morning, gym floor in the afternoon. You'll coach real sessions. Get real feedback. Make real mistakes in a safe environment.

 

This isn't theory. It's practice.
