Course Breakdown

Inside the Academy

This isn't a sit-and-listen course. Each week blends learning, application, and real feedback so you leave not just knowing what to do, but how to do it under pressure, in real coaching environments.

Week 1 — Foundations of Great Coaching

Set the standard. You'll learn the key systems, frameworks, and communication tools that define professional coaching, and start applying them from day one.

Week 2 — Communication, Cueing & Class Flow

It's not just what you know, it's how you deliver it. You'll refine how you see movement, cue efficiently, and manage individuals and groups with confidence.

Week 3 — Movement, Mechanics & Program Design

We go deep on biomechanics, energy systems, and smart programming. You'll build your ability to assess, design, and progress clients across any training goal.

Week 4 — Assessment & Certification

Your chance to put it all together. You'll be tested through practical coaching evaluations, a written exam, and a program design case study to earn your Bambu Training Academy certification.

Designed Around our 4C Pillars

Every module is built around the Bambu framework: Clarity, Communication, Control, and Care — the standards that guide how we coach, teach, and lead.
